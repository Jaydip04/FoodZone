          Service 3.5/5
        Ambience 3/5
        Food 3.5/5
        Value for Money: 4/5

        Being associated previously with Taj Group and now started with their own shops, this Bakery has been around for sometime already. It is located on the main road of Kemps Corner at 132, Damodar Mahal just besides Millionaire Clothes Store on the opposite side of Shalimar Hotel and is easy to spot although being a little bakery. They do not have valet parking and parking as such on this street is a problem so one needs to manage and find parking far off and walk to this bakery. The place is small and ambience is basic with no seating arrangements. The place serves vegetarian and non-vegetarian foods, muffins, sandwitches, cakes and pastries. They also have customised cake option available but one needs to pre-order the same.

        Ordered: Chocolate Croissant, Black Forest cake, Dutch Truffle Cake, Cuban Delight Cake

        Chocolate Croissant was decent and we loved the flavour. The Dutch Truffle was notably creamy but decent enough as well as the New York Chocolate Cake which was good too. We also tried Cuban Delight cake which was good where the chocolate was not too sweet and was perfect with mild butterscotch crunch.

        Bakery staff are good and well-mannered too. This bakery is open till 9pm so one can opt to go here for last minute cake orders and it’s a convenient place to rely on for quick snack at pocket friendly rates. Its value for money as compared to many similar taste pastry shops.

        Always hungry for more !!
